## What's New in OpenRCT2

OpenRCT2 improves on the original game with:

- **Increased limits**: Build larger parks with more rides, guests, and scenery
- **Improved AI**: Smarter guest and staff pathfinding and behavior
- **Quality of life**: Fast-forward, autosave, better editing tools, and more
- **Mod support**: Load custom objects, scenarios, and plugins
- **Open development**: Community-driven with regular updates and improvements
